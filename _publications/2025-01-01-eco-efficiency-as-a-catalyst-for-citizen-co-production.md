In our study, we explored the relationship between eco-efficiency and local governments engagement in collaborative environmental governance with citizens. By analyzing provincial-level data from China and employing advanced textual analysis methods, our findings reveal that localities with higher eco-efficiency are significantly more likely to adopt co-productive responses to environmental complaints.

The SSRN version is available [here](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=5220860).
